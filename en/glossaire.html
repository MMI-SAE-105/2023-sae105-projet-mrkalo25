Some complex words

Manhattan Project

The Manhattan Project was a secret programme during the Second World War to develop the first atomic bomb. which was finally used on Hiroshima and Nagasaki in 1945.

Oscars

The Oscars are renowned film awards presented each year to celebrate excellence in the film industry. the film industry.

Nominated

In cinema, a nominee is a candidate/film selected for an award such as an Oscar or Golden Globe. The nominees are in the running to win the award.
